Executive Summary

Why this matters

  • GP demand now averages 25.8 M appointments/month – 15 % above pre-COVID levels
  • Clinics operate at 115 % of planned capacity – no slack for shocks
  • 4.7 % of capacity is lost to missed appointments (DNA), mainly short-notice & remote bookings

Board-level actions

  1. Target DNA hotspots – SMS reminders for bookings ≤ 7 days
  2. Re-balance staffing – shift resources to high-demand ICBs & manage seasonal peaks
  3. Monitor sentiment – track hashtags #NHSCrisis, #ThankYouNHS

Tools & Techniques

Languages & Libraries: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-learn, Statsmodels, Plotly, Dash, Geopandas, TextBlob

Techniques Applied:

  • Descriptive statistics & exploratory data analysis (EDA)
  • Seasonal demand analysis with grouped bar charts
  • Logistic regression modelling DNA risk
  • ROC curve / AUC evaluation (AUC = 0.569)
  • Twitter sentiment analysis (TextBlob polarity)
  • Regex keyword & hashtag filtering
  • Utilisation mapping & regional comparison by ICB
  • Interactive dashboard prototype in JupyterDash
