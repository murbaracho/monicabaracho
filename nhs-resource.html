Business Problem

The NHS faces growing demand for GP services while operating with limited resources. Persistent challenges include:

  • Capacity strain: Many clinics operate above 100% of planned capacity, leaving little resilience for seasonal peaks or emergencies.
  • Missed appointments (DNA): Over a million no-shows each month cause significant avoidable costs and reduce access for other patients.
  • Regional inequalities: Some Integrated Care Boards (ICBs) remain persistently over-utilised, while others are under-utilised, creating disparities in patient access.

This project analyses NHS appointment data (Aug 2021 ‚Äì Jun 2022) to uncover utilisation patterns, missed appointment trends, and regional inequalities, providing actionable insights to support healthcare planning.
