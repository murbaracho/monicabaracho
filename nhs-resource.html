Executive summary

Context. GP appointment demand now averages 25.8 M per month—15 % above pre‑COVID levels—while clinics already operate at 115 % of planned capacity. Lost capacity from DNAs (~4.7 %) compounds the strain.

Key findings.

  • DNA risk spikes for same‑week telephone & video bookings.
  • Autumn 2021 hit 120 % utilisation — evidence of structural, not seasonal, overload.
  • Twitter sentiment mirrors pressure cycles (#NHSCrisis peaks during utilisation spikes).

Board‑level actions.

  1. Send SMS reminders for bookings ≤7 days.
  2. Reallocate staff to high‑demand ICBs and build surge rosters.
  3. Monitor sentiment hashtags weekly to flag emerging service‑trust issues.
